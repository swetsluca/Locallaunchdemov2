Our Story

Rotterdam's Street Food Legend

Born from the vibrant streets of Rotterdam, Sultan's Flame has been serving authentic Mediterranean street food since 2009. What started as a small family food stand has grown into one of the city's most beloved culinary destinations.

Our secret? Traditional recipes passed down through generations, the finest halal meats, and a commitment to quality that never wavers. Every dish is prepared with care, seasoned with our signature spice blend, and cooked over open flame to capture that unmistakable smoky flavor.
